Zero-knowledge proof verifier
Verify facts about a citizen's wealth without ever seeing their actual data. The system proves YES or NO — nothing more.
How ZKP works in BSC
Traditional system: Government asks "Show me your bank balance" and you must reveal ₹47,83,291. Privacy violated.
BSC ZKP: System asks "Does net worth exceed ₹1 crore?" Blockchain cryptographically proves YES or NO. Your actual balance is never revealed to anyone.
